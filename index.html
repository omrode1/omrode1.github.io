About

I am a BTech student in Electronics and Communications Engineering at St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, expected to graduate in 2024. I have excelled in my academic performance, earning multiple certifications and awards, such as the 1st runner-up position for the Intel Unnati Grand Challenge Winter 2022. I am also the Team Captain of Formula Ashwariders, a Formula Student team at my college, where I lead and manage a team of talented and passionate engineers who design and build race cars for the Formula Student competition. I have developed and implemented data acquisition systems for the team's vehicles, using C++, C, and MATLAB, and created and curated engaging content for the team's social media platforms. I have also secured sponsorships and managed finances, represented the team in external interactions, and ensured safety and compliance. I am passionate about exploring new technologies and participating in challenging and meaningful projects that can make a positive difference in the world. I have a keen interest and expertise in AI and machine learning, which I apply to various domains, such as aviation safety, driver assistance, and vehicle performance. I am confident that my skills and experience would be a valuable asset to any team. I am excited to continue learning and growing as an engineer and to contribute to innovative projects that can shape the future.
